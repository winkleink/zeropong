MCP30008    Raspberry Pi
VDD         3.3V
VREF        3.3V
AGND        GND
CLK         GPIO11 (P23)
DOUT        GPIO09 (P21)
DIN         GPIO10 (P19)
CS          GPIO8  (P24)
DGND        GND

The Analogue inputs on the MCP3008 are then:
CH0 - CH7
Pints 1 - 8
